Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we may collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Profile picture (if provided by Google)
  • Google Account identifier

We also collect financial information you voluntarily enter, including:

  • Income & expense records
  • Budget allocations and spending categories
  • Transaction history
  • Financial goals and related information

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and language settings
  • Usage statistics, crash reports, and performance data

C. Analytics Information

  • Screen views, feature usage, session duration
  • User engagement metrics
  • Diagnostic and performance information

D. Information We Never Collect

🔒 We do not access, collect, store, or process your banking passwords, debit card numbers, credit card numbers, UPI PINs, bank account credentials, or any other sensitive banking authentication information. Pocket Expense Manager is a manual expense tracking app — it does not connect to or interact with your bank or financial institution in any way.
